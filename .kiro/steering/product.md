# StudyMate Product Overview

StudyMate is a Vietnamese student networking platform that connects university students for collaborative learning through AI-powered matching, real-time messaging, and study rooms.

## Core Features

- **AI-Powered Matching**: Smart algorithm analyzes academic profiles to suggest compatible study partners based on subjects, interests, goals, and schedules
- **Real-time Messaging**: Chat system with file sharing, typing indicators, and voice/video calls using Pusher WebSocket technology
- **Study Rooms**: Voice/video chat rooms for group study sessions with screen sharing (premium feature)
- **Achievement System**: Gamified experience with badges, leaderboards, and progress tracking
- **University Verification**: Secure .edu email verification ensures student-only environment

## Target Users

Vietnamese university students seeking study partners and academic collaboration. Platform requires .edu email verification for access.

## Subscription Tiers

- **Basic (Free)**: 5 matches/day, 5 rooms/day
- **Premium (79k VND/month)**: Unlimited matches, advanced filters
- **Elite (149k VND/month)**: AI Tutor, exclusive events, career mentoring

## Key Metrics

- AI matching accuracy >85%
- Focus on student safety and academic collaboration
- Vietnamese language support with Inter font
